Anna Raymaker

ECE Ph.D. Candidate @ Georgia Tech

Hello! I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I work in the CPSec Lab, advised by Professor Saman Zonouz.
In May 2022, I acquired a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from the University of Florida HWCOE (Go Gators!).
My general research interests are in cyber-physical systems security, network security, and usable security.
If you want to get in touch, feel free to email or connect with me :)
